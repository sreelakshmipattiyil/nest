Explore Jammu and Kashmir

Discover the breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Jammu and Kashmir

Srinagar

Srinagar is famous for its picturesque Dal Lake, traditional houseboats, and the beautiful Mughal Gardens.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg, a popular hill station, is known for its stunning meadows, skiing resorts, and breathtaking views of the snow-capped mountains.

Leh-Ladakh

Leh-Ladakh is a region in Jammu and Kashmir known for its surreal landscapes, high-altitude deserts, and adventure activities like trekking and mountain biking.
