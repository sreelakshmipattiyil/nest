Explore Andhra Pradesh

Discover the cultural and natural wonders of Andhra Pradesh

Amaravati

Amaravati is an ancient city known for its Buddhist ruins and the construction of the new capital city of Andhra Pradesh.

Araku Valley

Araku Valley, a scenic hill station, is famous for its coffee plantations and picturesque landscapes in the Eastern Ghats.

Visakhapatnam

Known for its beautiful beaches, port city of Visakhapatnam also offers cultural landmarks like the Kailasagiri Hill and Submarine Museum.
