Explore Kerala

Enjoy the enchanting beauty of Kerala

Alleppey

Famous for its serene backwaters and houseboat cruises, Alleppey is a perfect spot to unwind and connect with nature.

Munnar

Known for its lush tea gardens and cool climate, Munnar offers a peaceful retreat in the Western Ghats.

Kumarakom

A tranquil village located along Vembanadu Lake, Kumarakom is famous for bird sanctuaries and houseboat cruises.

Wayanad

Wayanad is known for its wildlife, waterfalls, and scenic hills, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventurers.

Fort Kochi

Fort Kochi, with its colonial architecture and vibrant culture, is a historic place to visit in Kerala.
