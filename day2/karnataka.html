Explore Karnataka

Discover the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Karnataka

Coorg

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a scenic hill station known for its coffee plantations, waterfalls, and lush greenery.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hampi is an ancient village known for its historic ruins, temples, and monuments.

Chikmagalur

Chikmagalur is a beautiful hill station known for its coffee plantations, scenic views, and cool climate, making it a perfect getaway.
